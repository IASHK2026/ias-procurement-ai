FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who is this toolkit for?

It is designed for procurement professionals, buyers, sourcing specialists, importers and small procurement teams working with suppliers.

Do I need previous AI experience?

No. The prompts are structured for practical use and include clear placeholders that can be replaced with your own information.

Which AI tool can I use?

The prompts can be adapted for widely used text-based AI assistants. Always follow your organisation’s internal AI and data-protection policies.

Is this legal, compliance or financial advice?

No. The toolkit is provided for general informational and educational purposes. Users remain responsible for verifying outputs and following internal approval procedures.
